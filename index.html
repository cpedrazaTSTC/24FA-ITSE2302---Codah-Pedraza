Current School Status

As of today, I'm a sophomore in high school at South Texas ISD's Science Academy. Some notable achievements would be that I've kept straight A's and masters on state assessments for a couple years now. Along with this, I've been in the PTECH program since freshman year. I've had my ups and downs throughout the course, but at the end of the day its the career path I plan to fulfill as I inch closer to graduation. During senior year, we uphold an annual Engineering Design and Development event where all the seniors test and formulate a capstone project in correlation to solving a real-world problem. I intend to carry this out with my knowledge on computer science. Its all still to be determined, but with the use of Python and sensors, I could create a robot that could determine the difference between recyclable elements and trash. Usually, the winner of the competition is awarded with a scholarship and prize money. However, I still have time to truly decide on what I aspire to do during that time period.

How Did I Get Into Coding?

Since I was a young boy, technology has always been a distinct passion of mine, specifically coding. I've always wondered what happened behind a TV screen or phone, and how it contributed to its functionality. It was up until a couple years ago when I decided to finally answer that question. I first started coding in Python because it appeared straightforward, but now I'm familiar with various languages. These include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, C, and some CPP. Although C and CPP are equally as important as the others, I want to explore different scenarios and acquire new information from the languages we're learning today.
